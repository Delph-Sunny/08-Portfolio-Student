About Me

Hello! My name is Delphine and I am a full stack web developer student. I was born in France in a city nicknamed "The gate of the Alps". I learned how to ski as soon as I could walk and I still have a passion for anything related to winter sports. I also love travelling and meeting other cultures.

Working in the Localization field for years, I recently decided to extend my knowlege in computer science with a Coding Bootcamp at UConn. I am looking forward to apply my new skills at work.
