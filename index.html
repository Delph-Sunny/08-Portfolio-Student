About Me

My name is Delphine. I was born in France in a city nicknamed "The gate of the Alps". Growing up in a family of fanatic skiers, I learned how to ski as soon as I could walk. I wasn't thrilled with the cold at first, but the beauty of the big white mountains under a blue sky quickly won me over.

Like most kids in the area, I raced the local alpine ski races but as I got older, I started to appreciate backcountry more. Just before my freshman year in college, I tried snowboarding. I was hooked immediately. Weeks and vacations were not enough anymore. I found my new home and moved to Les 2 Alpes.

With a big domain, one of the best backcountries in the world, a glacier to ride in summer, a great nightlife and an active mountaineer community, this ski resort had everything I could dream of. Unfortunately, there wasn't much of a career opportunity outside of the ski/retail field so I had to move away once I graduated.

Now, with remote work being more popular in many companies, I may be able to move back there one day and once again gaze at the magnificent Muzelle out my window.
